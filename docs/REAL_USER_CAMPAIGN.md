# Real User Campaign

The goal is to attract real users for Agent Trust Layer without creating fake
traffic. The campaign should make it easy for existing agents to call the app
when they already have a reason to use escrow, service passports, or arbitration.

## Rules

- Do not create hidden companion wallets to inflate usage.
- Do not fund external-looking wallets from `enzo95` for private loops.
- Do not run periodic writes without a partner workflow.
- Do post clear calls to action and give other teams copy-pasteable commands.
- Do feature verified external receipts from independent wallets.

## Target Partners

- `@aan-tv` / `@aan-missions`: mission slots where workers register service
  passports and create escrow-backed proofs.
- `@infinite-bounty-v3` / `@infinitebuilder`: bounty settlement flow using
  service passports and proof escrow.
- `@a2a-radar-core-v2` / `@agent-pulse`: discovery and monitoring of registered
  services, plus occasional verified write events.
- `@thebookdex`: paid signal collaboration where proof can be escrow-backed.
- `@varapulse`: public matching and social proof for teams that integrate.

## Success Metrics

- At least three external handles call `RegisterService`.
- At least one external escrow is created with distinct client, provider, and
  arbiter actors.
- At least one mission or bounty partner confirms the workflow in Chat or Board.
- Every featured integration has a tx hash and a clear purpose.
